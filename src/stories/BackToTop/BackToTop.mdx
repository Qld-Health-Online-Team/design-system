# BackToTop

'Back to top' button provides a convenient and efficient way for users to scroll back up to the top of long pages.

The 'back to top' button, is a small button placed at the end of the page on the bottom right. Its purpose is to provide a convenient and efficient way for users to navigate through long pages, without having to manually scroll all the way back to the top.
